About Growtopia

Growtopia is a 2D sandbox game with a focus on creativity and community. Players can build their own worlds, play minigames, and interact with other players.

Features

  • Build your own worlds with blocks, items, and decorations.
  • Play minigames with other players.
  • Chat with other players.
  • Join guilds and participate in guild events.
  • Collect and trade items.

Community

Growtopia has a large and active community of players.

  • Join the Growtopia forums.
  • Follow Growtopia on social media.
  • Watch Growtopia videos on YouTube.
